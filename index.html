AutoVENT ABVM Firmware Upgrade Tool

Prerequisites

  • An up-to-date version of Chrome, at least version 61 or newer
  • An AUTOVENT BVM device

Instructions

  1. Plug ABVM into your computer or smartphone.
  2. Click "Connect to Device" and select the ABVM from the popup.
  3. Select firmware version and click "Upgrade Device"
